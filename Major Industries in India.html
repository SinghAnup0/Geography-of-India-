Q4: In which Indian city was the first successful Cotton mill set up?

Source: RRB NTPC 04.02.2021 (Shift-I) Stage Ist

The second cotton mill in India was established on 7 July, 1854 in Tardeo, in Bombay, British India. This mill is said to mark the true foundation of the modern cotton industry in India.
